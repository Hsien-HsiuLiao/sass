Why Two Choices of Sass Syntax?

With version 3, Sass syntax took two possible paths: the original Sass syntax, and the new .scss format. True to its name, .scss syntax is very similar to CSS: it uses curly braces and semicolons for declarations, whereas the original .sass syntax relies on indentation and line returns
